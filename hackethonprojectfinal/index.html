About EnergAI

EnergAI is a cutting-edge AI-based energy monitoring system designed to revolutionize how households and businesses manage energy consumption. Our mission is to empower individuals with real-time insights into their energy usage, enabling smarter decisions and fostering a sustainable future.With our innovative approach, we aim to reduce energy wastage, save costs, and contribute to a greener planet. EnergAI combines advanced AI algorithms, minimal hardware requirements, and user-friendly interfaces to provide detailed, appliance-level energy consumption data effortlessly.
